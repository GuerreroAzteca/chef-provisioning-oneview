HP OneView and Chef

End-to-end infrastructure to application automation

Eliminate infrastructure complexity with automation simplicity

HP OneView delivers simplified and consistent infrastructure management, unifying processes, user interface, and application programming interfaces (APIs) across multiple generations of HP compute, storage, and networking. HP OneView takes simplicity to the next level, by serving as an automation hub that allows other management tools—like Chef—to consume HP bare-metal infrastructure as code, and incorporate HP OneView templates and profiles into Chef recipes.

With Chef, you can automate how you build, deploy, and manage your infrastructure. Your infrastructure becomes as versionable, testable, and repeatable as application code.
